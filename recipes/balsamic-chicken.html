Balsamic Chicken

Description

"The tastiest chicken I've ever had." Me, 2022

Garlicy, balsamicy, chickeny, and ready in half an hour.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Tinned Tomatos
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Red Onions
  • Sweet Peppers
  • Oregano
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the red onion, sweet peppers, and fresh garlic.
  2. Fry the red onion in a little olive oil in the pan over a low heat until soft and slightly golden.
  3. Add the sweet peppers and fry for a couple of minutes. Add more olive oil if necessary.
  4. Add a table spoon of balsamic vinegar.
  5. Add the chicken thighs to the pan and increase to a medium heat. Fry until the skin begins to brown.
  6. Add another table spoon of balsamic vinegar.
  7. Add the fresh garlic and cook for a minute or two. Don't burn it! Add more olive oil if necessary.
  8. Add the oregano to the top of the mixture.
  9. Add the tinned Tomatos and turn down the heat.
  10. Add the water and leave it to reduce. Stir occasionally.
  11. Add more balsamic vinegar, and salt and pepper to taste.
