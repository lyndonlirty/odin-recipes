Beans on Toast

Description

"A great portion of beans on toast" - Me, 2022

Literally just beans on toast.

Ingredients

  • Baked Beans
  • Thick White Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pop a slice of bread into the toaster.
  2. Pop the beans in the microwave.
  3. Butter your toast.
  4. Pour the beans on top.
  5. Enjoy your beans on toast.
