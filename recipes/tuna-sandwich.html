Tuna Sandwich

Description

"That's lovely tuna, that" - Me, 2022

A bread and tuna classic.

Ingredients

  • Tinned Tuna
  • Thick White Bread
  • Butter
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Drain your tuna, then mix it with as much mayonaise as you desire.
  2. Butter two slices of bread.
  3. Put that tuna mayonaise mix inside your two slices of bread.
  4. Eat it.
